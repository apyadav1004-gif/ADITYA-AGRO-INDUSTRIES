Health Benefits

Sarson ke Tel ke
Fayde – Science aur Tradition

Our pure mustard oil is not just a cooking medium — it is a heritage of health, packed with nutrients nature intended.

❤️

Heart Health (Dil ke liye)

Rich in monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fatty acids (MUFA & PUFA), mustard oil helps maintain healthy cholesterol levels and supports cardiovascular function when used in balanced quantities.

🦠

Antibacterial & Antifungal

Contains allyl isothiocyanate which has natural antibacterial and antifungal properties — one reason our grandmothers used sarson ka tel for everything from cooking to skin care and massage.

💪

Joints & Muscles (Dard mein Rahat)

Traditional mustard oil massage (malish) improves blood circulation, reduces joint pain, and relieves muscle stiffness. Its warming effect has been trusted in Indian households for centuries.

🧠

Brain Function

High in omega-3 fatty acids (ALA), pure mustard oil contributes to healthy brain and nerve function, especially important for growing children and elderly members of the family.

🌿

Digestion & Metabolism (Pachan Sudhar)

Mustard oil stimulates digestive enzymes and bile production, improving digestion and metabolism. Its pungent components also help fight harmful gut bacteria naturally.

✨

Skin & Hair (Roop aur Baal)

Rich in Vitamin E, Omega-3 and natural antioxidants — pure mustard oil nourishes skin, promotes hair growth, prevents dandruff, and has been the secret of Indian beauty routines for generations.

🔥

High Smoke Point (Talna-Bhunna Perfect)

With a high smoke point (~480°F / 249°C), mustard oil is ideal for deep frying, stir-frying, tadka and high-heat cooking — making it the undisputed king of Indian kitchen oils.

🛡️

Immunity Booster (Rog Pratirodhak)

Regular use of pure mustard oil in cooking has been associated with improved immune response, thanks to its selenium, zinc, and antioxidant content that fight free radicals in the body.

🫁

Respiratory Benefits (Saans ki Taqleef mein)

Steam inhalation with mustard oil or its application on the chest is a traditional remedy for congestion, bronchitis, and cold. Its natural compounds have decongestant properties.

Our Production Process

1

Premium Seed Selection

Only high-quality sarson seeds sourced from trusted local UP farmers. No mixed or low-grade seeds.

2

Cleaning & Drying

Seeds are thoroughly cleaned, dried to optimal moisture level to remove impurities before pressing.

3

Cold Pressing (Kachi Ghani)

Low-heat expeller pressing at controlled temperature to extract pure oil while preserving all nutrients.

4

Natural Settling & Filtration

Oil is allowed to naturally settle and then filtered — no chemical refining, bleaching or deodorization.

5

Bottling & Quality Check

Sealed in food-grade containers after final quality verification and ready for dispatch to your doorstep.
